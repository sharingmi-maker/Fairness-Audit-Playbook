# Historical Context Assessment

## Purpose
To understand how historical, social, and organizational factors may introduce bias into AI systems.

## Required Actions
- Review historical human decisions or legacy systems
- Analyze historical data for demographic and intersectional disparities
- Identify stakeholders affected by past inequities

## Intersectional Fairness
Assess historical outcomes across combinations of attributes (e.g., age + gender + ethnicity).

## Output
A documented context assessment that informs fairness definition selection.
