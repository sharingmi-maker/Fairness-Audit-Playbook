# Fairness Definition Selection

## Purpose
To define what fairness means for the specific AI system.

## Common Definitions
- Demographic Parity
- Equal Opportunity
- Equalized Odds
- Predictive Parity
- Individual Fairness

## Required Actions
- Select fairness definitions based on system goals and risks
- Document trade-offs and constraints

## Intersectional Fairness
Apply fairness definitions across intersectional demographic groups.

## Output
A fairness definition decision log.
